Problem Users Face

People often waste time deciding what to cook, especially when they only have a few random ingredients. Beginners feel stuck, busy users skip meals, and students default to unhealthy quick options.

ChefyAI Solution Overview

ChefyAI solves this by turning ingredients into curated recipes, guiding users step by step, and keeping favorites in one place. It makes meal decisions faster and easier.
