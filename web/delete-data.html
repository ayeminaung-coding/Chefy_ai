Chefy AI

Data Deletion Request

Effective date: 11 April 2026

This page explains how users of Chefy AI can request deletion of data without deleting their account.

1. How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to 6731503047@lamduan.mfu.ac.th.
  2. Use subject: Chefy AI Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include your account email and what data you want deleted.

2. Data You Can Request to Delete

  • Saved favorites data linked to your account.
  • Associated profile information used in the app (where applicable).

3. Data That May Be Retained

  • Minimal technical records required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
  • Data retained by third-party providers according to their legal obligations.

4. Retention Timeline

We process deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible. Typical completion time is within 30 days from verification of the request.
